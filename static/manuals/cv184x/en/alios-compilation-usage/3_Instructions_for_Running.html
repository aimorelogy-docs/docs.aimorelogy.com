3. Running Instructions¶

  1. After burning is complete, turn off the power and wait 1-2 seconds for the capacitors to discharge.

  2. The ###YOC### message printed after power-on indicates that the kernel has finished booting.

  1. Press Enter in the serial console. Normal input/output and cli_uart output indicate that the system is running normally.

  1. Using the AliOS virtual serial port requires configuring two locations during compilation

  1. To view the small-core PC value and observe it changing: devmem 0x1901070
